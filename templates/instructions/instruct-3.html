Trial Types

3 of 3

There are three types of trials in the game. Before each trial begins, a label will appear indicating the type of upcoming trial. The types of trials are "Single", "Double", and "Press", and each is described below. In all trials, the brighter the reward region, the more valuable it is. Reward values range between $0.01 and $1.00, with $1.00 being the brightest. You will be told what the exact value of your selection is immediately after clicking on it (for example, $0.75).

While Single and Double trials start automatically, Press trials give you two seconds to press the spacebar key if you would like to keep the colors on the reward regions. Otherwise, they will both be the same shade of gray. The number of presses required is random.
