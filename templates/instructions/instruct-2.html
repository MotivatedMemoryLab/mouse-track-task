The Cursor

2 of 3

The cursor in this game has special properties:

  • It starts at the bottom of the screen every trial.
  • It looks like a black ring, which replaces the normal cursor.
  • It moves much slower than normal to elicit a stronger sense of motivation.

This requires certain things of you:

  • Move your mouse back to near your body in between trials, since the cursor will move to the bottom of the screen automatically.
  • Make sure you have enough room (approx. 1 to 2 sq. ft.) to move the mouse from the bottom of the screen to the top left / right without picking it up. Make this adjustment during the practice trials.
  • Do not exit the mouse replacement and regain control of your mouse outside specified breaks. Here are some ways that might happen:
    • Pressing the 'escape' key
    • Switching to another window or pressing 'Alt' + 'Tab'
    • Pressing the 'back' button on your mouse or keyboard
    If you exit during the main experiment, your results will be invalid and your bonus forfeited.
